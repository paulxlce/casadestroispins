Effortless comfort

Designed for families and friends, Casa des Trois Pins is a home where you feel good immediately. Generous volumes, fluid circulation, and abundant light.

With 6 air‑conditioned bedrooms and 5 bathrooms, everyone has their own space without getting in each other’s way. The large, light‑filled living room naturally becomes the heart of the house: you gather, you share, you slow down.

Outside, two terraces with different atmospheres let you live with the day, between sun and shade. The heated pool extends relaxing moments from morning to the calmer end of day.
